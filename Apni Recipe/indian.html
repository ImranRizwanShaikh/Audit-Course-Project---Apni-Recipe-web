Samosa

Ingredients

  1. 3 Tbsp(26.0 gm) Wheat Flour (Whole)
  2. 1/4 Diced Cup(42.0 gm) Potato
  3. 1 Tbsp(10.0 gm) Peas (Boiled)
  4. 1 Tsp(0.92 gm) Coriander Leaves (Chopped)
  5. 1/2 Tsp(3.0 gm) Ginger Garlic Paste
  6. 1/8 Tsp(0.29 gm) Ajwain
  7. 1/8 Tsp(0.27 gm) Red Chilli (Powder)
  8. 0.13 Tsp(0.18 gm) Garam Masala
  9. 1/8 Tsp(0.21 gm) Dhania Powder
  10. 1/8 Tsp(0.3 gm) Haldi
  11. 1/3 Tsp(1.0 gm) Salt
  12. 2.0 Tbsp(16.0 ml) Oil
  13. 50.0 Ml(50.0 ml) Water


Recipe

  1. In a bowl add 1/2 cup boiled diced potato.
  2. To the potatoes add 1/8 tsp salt, haldi, red chilli powder, dhania powder, garam masala, ajwain, 1/2 tsp ginger garlic paste and 1 tsp chopped coriander leaves.
  3. Mash and mix well.
  4. Now add 1 tbsp of boiled peas and mix it well again.
  5. Roll the dough and fill the potato peas stuffing.
  6. Cut the rolled dough into triangular shape, fold into samosa shape and deep fry.
  7. Serve hot with chutneys.
